My Art



Welcome to the fun zone!

Our art class is hands-on, noisy with giggles, and bursting with color. Some projects stretch your brain and that is the exciting part. Because the payoff is a beautiful painting you can be proud of. Looking back through all your creations feels like scrolling through warm fun memories. I paint with Camel Poster Colours. They are great for watery mixes and bold finishes. The magical part is the outline: add that last line and poof. The whole scene pops! How we make the magic:
1- Draw the frame - pick where your adventure will live on the paper.
2- Sketch - give your idea a light pencil hug.
3- Paint - splash, blend, layer — play with color!
4- Outline - the final flourish that brings your painting to life.
